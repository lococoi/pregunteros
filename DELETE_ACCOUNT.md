# Account and Data Deletion Instructions – Pregunteros

If you wish to permanently delete your account and associated data, you
may do so by submitting a request via email.

---

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to:

[lococoi@proton.me](mailto:lococoi@proton.me)

Subject line:

# Account Deletion Request – Pregunteros

The request must be sent from the same email address associated with
your Pregunteros account.

---

# Required Information

In the body of your email, please include:

-   The email address associated with your account
-   An explicit statement confirming that you wish to permanently delete
    your account

No additional information will be requested unless strictly necessary to
verify account ownership.

---

# What Happens After Deletion

Once your request is processed:

-   Your account will be permanently deleted
-   Your encrypted email address and encrypted password will be removed
-   Quizzes and responses associated with your profile may be deleted
-   No personally identifiable information will be retained

Account deletion is irreversible.

---

# Processing Time

Deletion requests will be processed within a maximum of 7 business days
from receipt.

You may receive a confirmation email once the deletion process has been
completed.

---

# Third-Party Managed Data

Please note:

-   Advertising-related data (Google AdMob) is managed by Google.
-   Subscription and payment data (Google Play Billing) is managed by
    Google Play.

To manage or delete such data, you must contact Google directly in
accordance with their privacy policies.
